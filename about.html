About Me

Learn more about my background, interests, and experiences below!

Short Bio

I grew up in Holden, Maine as the fourth of five children. It was a smaller community, so I really grew close to the people I knew growing up and learned to cherish relationships. In my youth, I developed a love for sports and music. I played basketball, baseball, soccer, and tennis and was heavily involved in the Jazz band program at my high school, playing the trumpet.

Young Adult Life

After graduating, I spent a year at Brigham Young University in Utah before serving for two years as a missionary for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints. My missionary service was spent in Guatemala, and even though it was a little difficult to grasp the language at first, I loved the experience helping the people there grow closer to God and improve their lives. I was in Guatemala from June of 2023 until June of 2025.

Current Life and Interests

I currently still am involved in sports casually; I love a pick-up game. I also enjoy swimming as well as skiing and just being an active member of the community. College life is great, because there is always a performance to see or a game to cheer at.

I am an undergrad at Brigham Young University (BYU) studying Business and am thinking of going into Supply Chain or possibly Information Systems. I will graduate in April 2028.
